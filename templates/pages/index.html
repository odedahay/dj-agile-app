More About AGILE

Ship manning

We supply top calibre Filipino officers and highly skilled ratings to all types of tankers. We grow our own officers from our pool of deck and engine cadets. Our petty officers and ratings go through continuous tooling and re-tooling trainings to level up their skills and imbibe safety culture.

Crew management

We are proud of more than 15 years of experience managing the Filipino crew complement for LNG ships. Judicious budget management without compromising the quality of the living and working conditions of the Filipino crew onboard is our guiding principle.
